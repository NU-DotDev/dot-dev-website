FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to be a Computer Science major to join .dev?

A: No- students from any major are welcome to join.

Q: What if I am not an experienced programmer?

A: Not a problem- our project competition and competitive programming group provide many opportunities to learn.

Q: Is .dev still hosting events during the ongoing pandemic?

A: Yes- for the safety of our members, all events are now virtual, but will still occur as planned.
